Profits in the 50 States

Profits in each of the 50 states are looking interesting. While the poor fellows in some states, most notably Texas, Ohio, and Pennsylvania, have negative profits, other states made bank. California, New York, and Washington made the heftiest sums.

 

Profits in California in the Last Two Years

In the state of California, profits have been all over the place during the last two years. While they have never gone negative, they have had negative profit growth. Their highest profit sat at $4,235 in November 2013.

 

Product Category Profits in California

Within the state of California, Hewlett Packard, Canon, and Fellowes lead the pack for profits within each product category.

 

Hewlett Packard in California

The most profitable category in California was the Hewlett Packard Laser Jet. Unfortunately, there was not enough data in any of the categories from California, including this one, for a forecast. However, had there been enough data, it would have forecasted through November 2015 and I would have done this by right clicking on the finished graph and clicking "Forecast" --> "Show Forecast".>
